Account Deletion Policy for Cashew App

We respect your right to control your personal information. You can request to delete your account and personal data at any time. However, please note that some data may be retained for legal or technical reasons.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request account deletion, please contact us at apps@apollotech.co. We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you delete your account, we will take the following actions:

  • Permanently delete your personal information, including your name, email address, and any other data you have provided to us.
  • Remove your account from our systems.
  • Delete any content you have created within the app, such as posts, comments, or messages.

Data Retention

We may retain certain data even after you delete your account, including:

  • Data required for legal compliance, such as records of financial transactions.
  • Data needed for technical reasons, such as preventing fraud or abuse.
  • Aggregated or anonymized data that cannot be used to identify you.

Additional Information

If you have any questions about this Account Deletion Policy, please contact us at apps@apollotech.co.
